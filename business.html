For Business Users

Get discovered

By signing up for CliqSpot, your business will be added to our suggestion list for events in your area, helping you get discovered by new customers who are looking to plan fun and engaging outings.

Boost your visibility

With our priority-based system, businesses with higher priority levels will appear at the top of the suggestion list, ensuring maximum visibility for your brand.

Increase engagement

By participating in CliqSpot, you'll become part of a vibrant community of local businesses and customers, helping you increase engagement and build lasting relationships with your customers.

Improve your marketing strategy

By using CliqSpot's analytics tools, you can gain valuable insights into your customers' behavior and preferences. This information can help you better understand your target audience.
